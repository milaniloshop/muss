# content_pillars.md — Content themes

> The 4 themes Milan Hype CoreFit owns. Use for calendars, social, email, SEO, and campaign planning.
> Last updated: 2026-07-13

---

## Mix (default)

| Pillar | Mix | Goal |
|--------|-----|------|
| 1. Silhouette Confidence | 35% | Demand + brand memory |
| 2. Real Compression (Anti-Gimmick) | 25% | Trust + differentiation |
| 3. Tier Engineering | 25% | Education → conversion |
| 4. Under Every Shirt (Lifestyle) | 15% | Aspiration + retention |

Adjust mix for launches (more Tier Engineering) or trust crises (more Anti-Gimmick).

---

## Pillar 1 — Silhouette Confidence

**Owns:** How clothes look and feel on a man’s body after CoreFit.

**Topics / clusters**
- Fitted tee problem → flatter chest contour
- Dress shirt / office confidence
- Date night / evening silhouette
- “Clothes you already own look better”
- Before/after framing without body-shaming (focus on shirt drape, not weight)

**Formats:** hero ads, PDP openers, Reels (shirt-on reveal), email subject lines  
**CTA bias:** Shop CoreFit / Find Your Tier

---

## Pillar 2 — Real Compression (Anti-Gimmick)

**Owns:** Category truth. We are not fake muscle.

**Topics / clusters**
- Compression vs pads vs “abs shirts”
- What CoreFit does / does not do (FAQ fuel)
- Discreet packaging and shopping dignity
- Honest reviews and sizing notes
- “Not padded. Not fake muscle.” campaign lines

**Formats:** FAQ carousels, myth-bust posts, comparison tables, support macros  
**CTA bias:** Learn the difference / Start with Essential or Pro

---

## Pillar 3 — Tier Engineering

**Owns:** Why four tiers exist and how to choose.

**Topics / clusters**
- Essential — daily entry, moderate hold, $49
- Pro — best seller, firm hold, Italian microfiber, silver-ion
- Elite — precision mapping, Japanese cooling yarn, travel/heat
- Signature — maximum hold, Swiss-weave, gold-thread, black-tie
- Zone education (chest / core / sides / cooling / gold seam)
- Fabric care and longevity

**Formats:** collection page modules, comparison charts, 3D/product explainer, email nurture  
**CTA bias:** Compare Tiers / Shop Pro (default recommendation)

---

## Pillar 4 — Under Every Shirt (Lifestyle)

**Owns:** Context of wear — work, travel, gym-to-dinner, formal.

**Topics / clusters**
- Invisible under polos, tees, knits, dress shirts
- Travel days and long wear (odor / moisture)
- Event / black-tie (Signature)
- Warm climate comfort (Elite)
- Pairing notes (style it) without turning into a full fashion magazine

**Formats:** lifestyle stills, short lifestyle video, lookbook strips below the fold  
**CTA bias:** Shop the look’s tier / Complete the set (multi-pack when offered)

---

## Pillar criteria (quality bar)

A piece belongs in CoreFit content if it:

1. Aligns with compression / silhouette confidence (not generic gym motivation)
2. Matches ICP pains in `audience.md`
3. Stays inside `brand_voice.md` rules
4. Can point to a real product fact from `website/lib/products.ts`
