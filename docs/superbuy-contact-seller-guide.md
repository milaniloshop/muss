# How to Contact Seller on Superbuy

Ask about **Milan Hype custom logo / branding** using one of these 3 ways.

---

## Method 1 — BEFORE you pay (easiest)

On the **product page** where you picked Black + White:

1. Scroll down near **Buy Now** / **Add to Cart**
2. Look for **"Remark"** or **"Notes"** or **"Product Notes"**
3. Paste this:

```
Hi, US brand Milan Hype. Can you add our brand label on the product? What MOQ for custom logo and packaging?
```

4. Then click **Buy Now**

Superbuy staff reads this when they buy from the Taobao seller.

---

## Method 2 — AFTER you ordered (best)

1. Go to: **https://www.superbuy.com/en/page/account/orders/**
   - Or: Superbuy → **User Center** → **My Orders**
2. Find your compression tank order
3. Click **"Order Inquiry"** or **"Inquiry"** (blue link/button on that order)
4. Paste in **English**:

```
Please contact the seller for me:
- Can they add custom brand label "Milan Hype" on the tank?
- What is minimum order quantity for custom logo?
- Can they customize packaging box or bag?
Thank you.
```

Superbuy translates to Chinese and sends to the seller. They reply in English.

---

## Method 3 — Live chat (anytime)

1. Bottom-right corner of Superbuy → **Online Service** / chat bubble
2. Hours: **9:00–18:00 Beijing time** (check your local time)
3. Say: *"Please ask my seller about custom Milan Hype branding on order [your order number]"*

Email backup: **service@superbuy.com** (include order number)

---

## What to expect back

| Question | Likely answer |
|----------|----------------|
| Logo on 2 sample pieces? | No |
| Custom branding MOQ? | 50–500 pieces |
| Still ship samples? | Yes |

**Order samples anyway** — branding comes later at bulk.
