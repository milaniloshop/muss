## Good listing checklist

- **Fabric:** 80–90% nylon, 10–20% spandex (锦纶/氨纶)
- **Style:** Sleeveless tank, crew or low neck
- **Colors:** Black + white
- **Sizes:** S–XXL
- **No** foam chest pads
- **Price:** ~¥15–40/unit bulk ($2–6) is normal

## Red flags — skip listing

- 肌肉衣 / 假肌肉 (fake muscle)
- 海绵垫 (foam pads)
- 6-pack printed abs
- "Instant muscle"
- Less than 70% nylon
