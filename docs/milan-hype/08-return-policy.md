## Return Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

---

### Our Promise

We want you to love every Milan Hype piece. If something isn't right, we make returns straightforward — because premium fashion should come with premium service.

---

### 30-Day Return Window

You have **30 days** from the date of delivery to return any eligible item for a refund or exchange.

---

### Eligible Items

To be eligible for a return, items must be:

- **Unworn** and in the same condition as received
- **Unwashed** and free of odors, stains, or damage
- **Tags attached** — all original tags must be intact
- **In original packaging** (if applicable)
- Returned within **30 days** of delivery

---

### Non-Returnable Items

The following cannot be returned:

- Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
- Items without original tags
- Items marked as **Final Sale**
- Items returned after the 30-day window
- Items that show signs of use, damage, or wear

---

### How to Start a Return

1. Email **support@milanhype.com** with:
   - Your order number
   - Item(s) you wish to return
   - Reason for return (optional — helps us improve)
2. Our team will respond within **24 hours** with return instructions
3. Pack the item securely with tags attached
4. Ship the item back using the provided instructions
5. Once received and inspected, your refund or exchange will be processed

---

### Return Shipping

- **Standard returns:** Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer
- **Damaged, defective, or incorrect items:** Milan Hype covers return shipping and will send a prepaid label or reimburse your return cost

We recommend using a trackable shipping method. Milan Hype is not responsible for returns lost in transit.

---

### Refunds

- Refunds are issued to the **original payment method**
- Processing time: **5–10 business days** after we receive and inspect your return
- Original shipping charges are **non-refundable** (unless the return is due to our error)
- You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed

---

### Exchanges

Want a different size or color? We'd love to help.

1. Email **support@milanhype.com** with your order number and the size/color you'd like
2. If the item is in stock, we'll arrange the exchange
3. If the item is out of stock, we'll issue a full refund so you can reorder when it's available

**Note:** Exchanges are subject to availability. We recommend returning for a refund and placing a new order if you need a different item quickly.

---

### Damaged or Incorrect Items

Received the wrong item or something damaged? We're sorry — that shouldn't happen.

1. Email **support@milanhype.com** within **48 hours** of delivery
2. Include your order number and photos of the issue
3. We'll send a replacement at no extra cost or issue a full refund — your choice
4. We'll cover all return shipping for damaged or incorrect items

---

### Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within 10 business days of our confirmation email:

1. Check your bank account or credit card statement
2. Contact your bank — processing times vary
3. If you've done both, email **support@milanhype.com**

---

### Sale Items

Items purchased on sale can be returned within the standard 30-day window unless marked as **Final Sale**. Final Sale items are non-returnable and non-refundable.
