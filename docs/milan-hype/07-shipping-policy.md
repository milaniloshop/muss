## Shipping Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

---

### Overview

At Milan Hype, we want your order to arrive quickly and safely. This policy outlines our shipping rates, timeframes, and what to expect after you place your order.

---

### Shipping Rates — United States

| Order Total | Shipping Cost | Method |
|-------------|---------------|--------|
| **$75 and above** | **FREE** | Standard Shipping |
| **Below $75** | **$6.95** | Standard Shipping |

Shipping rates are calculated automatically at checkout based on your order subtotal (before tax).

---

### Processing Time

All orders are processed within **1–2 business days** (Monday–Friday, excluding US federal holidays).

Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

You'll receive a confirmation email when your order is placed and a shipping confirmation with tracking when your order ships.

---

### Delivery Time

| Method | Estimated Delivery |
|--------|-------------------|
| Standard Shipping (US) | 3–7 business days after shipment |

Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on your location and carrier conditions. Milan Hype is not responsible for delays caused by the carrier, weather, or other events outside our control.

---

### Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with:
- Carrier name (USPS or UPS)
- Tracking number
- Link to track your package

If you haven't received a shipping confirmation within 3 business days of ordering, contact us at **support@milanhype.com**.

---

### Shipping Address

Please ensure your shipping address is correct at checkout. We are not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

If you notice an error after placing your order, contact us at **support@milanhype.com** within **1 hour** and we'll do our best to update it before shipment.

---

### Lost or Stolen Packages

If your tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your package:
1. Check with neighbors, building management, or household members
2. Contact the carrier directly with your tracking number
3. Email us at **support@milanhype.com** within 7 days of the marked delivery date

We'll work with you and the carrier to resolve the issue. If the package is confirmed lost, we'll send a replacement or issue a full refund.

---

### Damaged in Transit

If your order arrives damaged:
1. Take photos of the damage (packaging and product)
2. Email **support@milanhype.com** within 48 hours of delivery
3. Include your order number and photos

We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund — your choice.

---

### International Shipping

Milan Hype currently ships to the **United States only**. International shipping is not available at this time. Follow [@milanhype](https://instagram.com/milanhype) for updates on international expansion.
