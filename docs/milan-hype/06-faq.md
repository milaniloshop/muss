## Orders & Shipping

### How long does shipping take?

Orders are processed within **1–2 business days**. Once shipped, standard US delivery typically takes **3–7 business days**. You'll receive a tracking link by email as soon as your order is on the way.

### How much does shipping cost?

- **Free standard shipping** on all US orders over **$75**
- **$6.95 flat rate** for orders under $75

Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your order total.

### Do you ship internationally?

At this time, Milan Hype ships within the **United States only**. International shipping is coming soon — follow [@milanhype](https://instagram.com/milanhype) for updates.

### How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also reach out to us at **support@milanhype.com** with your order number and we'll get you an update.

### Can I change or cancel my order?

We process orders quickly to get your items to you as soon as possible. If you need to change or cancel your order, contact us at **support@milanhype.com** within **1 hour** of placing it. We'll do our best to help, but we can't guarantee changes once processing has begun.

### What carriers do you use?

We ship via USPS and UPS for standard US delivery. Your tracking email will include the carrier and tracking number.

## Returns & Exchanges

### What is your return policy?

We offer **easy 30-day returns** on unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached. Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery.

### How do I start a return?

Email us at **support@milanhype.com** with your order number and the item(s) you'd like to return. We'll send you return instructions within 24 hours.

### Are returns free?

Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer unless the item is **damaged, defective, or incorrect** — in those cases, we'll cover return shipping and send a replacement or full refund.

### How long do refunds take?

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within **5–10 business days** to your original payment method.

### Can I exchange for a different size?

Yes. Contact us at **support@milanhype.com** with your order number and the size you'd like. If the new size is in stock, we'll arrange the exchange. If not, we'll issue a refund so you can reorder.

### What items cannot be returned?

- Items worn, washed, or altered
- Items without original tags
- Items marked as final sale
- Items returned after 30 days

## Sizing & Products

### How do I find my size?

Each product page includes **fit notes** and a link to our [Size Guide](/pages/size-guide). If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for a relaxed fit.

### What if my item doesn't fit?

No problem. We offer easy 30-day returns. Order the size you think works best, try it on, and return it if the fit isn't right.

### How do I care for my garments?

Care instructions are listed on each product page under Details. Following these instructions helps maintain fit, color, and quality over time.

### Will sold-out items be restocked?

Some items are restocked based on demand. Follow [@milanhype](https://instagram.com/milanhype) or join our email list for restock announcements. Limited-run pieces may not return.

### Are your product photos accurate?

We photograph every item in professional lighting on white backgrounds and on models. Colors may vary slightly depending on your screen settings. If your item looks significantly different from the photos, contact us — we'll make it right.

## Payment & Security

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, Shop Pay, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal (if enabled).

### Is my payment information secure?

Yes. Milan Hype uses Shopify's secure checkout with SSL encryption. We never store your full payment details.

### Can I use a discount code?

Yes. Enter your code at checkout in the discount field. Only one code can be applied per order. Discount codes cannot be combined with sale items unless stated otherwise.

## Account & Communication

### Do I need an account to order?

No. Guest checkout is available. Creating an account lets you track orders and check out faster on future purchases.

### How do I join the email list?

Subscribe via the newsletter form on our homepage or at checkout. You'll get first access to new drops and exclusive offers.

### How do I unsubscribe from emails?

Click the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any marketing email. You can also email us at **support@milanhype.com** and we'll remove you.

### How do I opt out of SMS?

Reply **STOP** to any SMS from Milan Hype, or email us at **support@milanhype.com**.
