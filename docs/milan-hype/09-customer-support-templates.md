# Milan Hype — Customer Support SOPs & Templates

**Support email:** support@milanhype.com  
**Instagram:** [@Milanhype_](https://instagram.com/Milanhype_)  
**Response SLA:** Within 24 hours (Monday–Friday)  
**Tone:** Professional · Friendly · Helpful · Brand-focused

---

## 1. Support Workflow

```
Customer contacts → Categorize ticket → Respond with template (personalized) → Resolve → Close
                                              ↓
                                    Escalate if unresolved after 48hr
```

### Ticket Categories

| Category | Priority | SLA |
|----------|----------|-----|
| Damaged / wrong item | Urgent | 4 hours |
| Order not received (past delivery date) | High | 12 hours |
| Return / refund request | Standard | 24 hours |
| Tracking inquiry | Standard | 24 hours |
| Sizing question | Standard | 24 hours |
| General inquiry | Standard | 24 hours |
| Complaint | High | 12 hours |
| Collaboration / press | Low | 48 hours |

---

## 2. Response Rules

1. **Always** use the customer's first name
2. **Always** include order number when applicable
3. **Never** use copy-paste without personalizing at least one line
4. **Never** argue with the customer — acknowledge, solve, follow up
5. **Sign off** as "Milan Hype Support" or with agent first name
6. **Instagram DMs:** Keep shorter than email, link to email for complex issues

## 5. Escalation Protocol

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Customer threatens chargeback | Resolve immediately — refund or replace, no debate |
| Media / legal threat | Escalate to brand owner, respond within 4 hours |
| 3+ contacts about same issue | Escalate + offer goodwill gesture |
| Damaged item + angry customer | Refund + 10% off next order code |
| Unresolved after 48 hours | Personal email from brand owner |
