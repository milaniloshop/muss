## Product FAQs

**Q: How does this fit?**  
A: This low-top fits true to size on a standard foot. If you have wide feet or prefer extra room, size up half a size.

**Q: Are the pins removable?**  
A: The enamel pins are hand-placed and can be removed carefully if you prefer a cleaner look. We recommend keeping them on for the full designed aesthetic.

**Q: Is every pair exactly the same?**  
A: The jacquard pattern placement may vary slightly between pairs due to the woven construction. Pin placement is consistent. This is part of the artisan character of the shoe.

**Q: How do I clean these?**  
A: Spot clean the jacquard upper with a soft brush or damp cloth. Do not submerge or machine wash. Wipe leather sections with a leather-safe cleaner.

**Q: Is this a limited drop?**  
A: Yes. This is a limited-run style. Once sold out, it may not return.

**Q: Can I return these if they don't fit?**  
A: Yes — unworn, in original condition with box, within 30 days. See our Return Policy for full details.
