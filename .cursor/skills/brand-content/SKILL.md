# Brand Content — Milan Hype CoreFit

Source-of-truth marketing docs for **Milan Hype** / product line **CoreFit** (men's chest + core compression tanks).

## When to Use

- Writing or rewriting site copy, PDPs, emails, ads, social, SMS
- Campaign briefs, creative direction, or image prompts
- Checking tone, CTAs, offers, or audience fit
- Generating content ideas from pillars

## How to Use

1. Read the relevant reference file(s) below before drafting.
2. Match voice rules in `brand_voice.md` — never invent slang or fake-muscle claims.
3. Ground offers and CTAs in `offers_cta.md` (tier ladder Essential → Signature).
4. For visuals / Nano Banana / design, follow `graphics_brief.md` + `website/design-system/MASTER.md`.
5. Prefer real product facts from `website/lib/products.ts` over generic fitness marketing.

## Hard Rules

- **Product truth:** Compression that flattens/smooths. Not padded. Not fake muscle. Not abs gimmicks.
- **Brand first:** Milan Hype / CoreFit must lead premium surfaces; avoid generic gym-bro voice.
- **One CTA** per surface unless the design system already shows a primary + secondary pair.
- **Dark luxury** visual language — charcoal/black/silver/gold — not purple gradients or cream broadsheet looks.
